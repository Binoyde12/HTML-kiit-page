About KIIT Bhubaneswar

KIIT University is a private university located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. It was founded in 1992 by Achyuta Samanta as the Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), offering only Diploma Engineering programmes. Today, it has 28 constituent schools offering undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various fields such as engineering, law, management, medicine, computer application, fashion, film, television, media and journalism, and humanities. The university also has a research and development wing, KIIT-TBI, which incubates startup companies in various fields.
